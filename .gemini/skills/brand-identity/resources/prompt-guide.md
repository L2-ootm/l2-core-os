# L2 SYSTEMS // IMAGE GENERATION GUIDE

> **Aesthetic:** Dark Luxury Industrialism / Hybrid Monolith.
> **Philosophy:** "The Camera is inside the machine."

When generating images for L2, follow these constraints strictly.

## 1. The Subject
*   **Physical:** Heavy machinery, server racks, obsidian monoliths, glass data crystals.
*   **Abstract:** Geodesic structures, wireframes floating in void, electric arcs.
*   **Forbidden:** Humans faces (unless obscured by helmets), clouds, trees, generic office stock photos.

## 2. The Materials
Always specify these materials in your prompt:
*   `Matte Black Anodized Aluminum` (The frame).
*   `Semi-transparent Purple Glass` (The data).
*   `Brushed Titanium` (The accents).

## 3. The Lighting
*   **Type:** `Cinematic rim lighting`, `Volumetric fog`, `Low key`.
*   **Colors:** Deep shadows (#050505) punctuated by `Electric Violet` lasers or `Cyber Blue` status lights.

## 4. The Camera
*   **Lens:** `Macro 100mm` (for details) or `Wide Angle 24mm` (for environments).
*   **Depth of Field:** Very shallow (f/1.8). Focus on the "hero" element, blur the rest.
*   **Quality:** `8k resolution`, `Unreal Engine 5 render style`, `Raytracing`.

## 5. Example Prompt Template
*"A close-up macro shot of a [SUBJECT]. Made of [MATERIAL 1] and [MATERIAL 2]. [LIGHTING] lighting with beams of [COLOR] light cutting through volumetric fog. High contrast, sharp focus, 8k, cinematic, brutalist design."*
