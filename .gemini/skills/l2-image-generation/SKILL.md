## ⚡ The 50ms Rule (Neuroaesthetics)

The brain judges an image in **50 milliseconds**. To stop the scroll, you must trigger a "Prediction Error".

### 1. The Red Strategy (Chromatic Imperative)
-   **Why:** Red/Orange wavelengths focus *behind* the retina, creating a physical pseudo-3D effect ("Advancing Color").
-   **Usage:** Use **Electric Violet (#7F00FF)** and **Deep Crimson/Red** accents against **Obsidian (#050505)** backgrounds.
-   **Fix:** Reduce Red saturation by 5-10% to prevent JPG compression artifacts.

### 2. High-Frequency Data
-   Images must be **SHARP**. Use keywords: `8k`, `macro photography`, `hyper-detailed`, `pores`, `surface imperfections`.
-   **Avoid:** Smooth surfaces, plastic looks, flat colors.

## 🖼️ The P.R.O.M.P.T. Protocol (JSON)

**STOP.** Do not write sentences. Write **JSON**.
Natural language bleeds concepts. JSON isolates variables.

### The Formula: `FRAME` Framework

1.  **F (Focus):** Identity Lock (Face/Object).
2.  **R (Radiance):** Lighting Strategy.
3.  **A (Angle):** Camera Hierarchy.
4.  **M (Matter):** Textures & Physics.
5.  **E (Extras):** Action & Props.

## 🎨 Design Benchmarks (The "Vanguards")

When in doubt, align with these styles:

*   **Rafa Franco:** "Haptic Visuality" (Text interwoven with physical objects).
*   **Agencia Setup:** "Meta-Design" (High-pass filter sharpness, kinetic distortion).
*   **Mazzei Design:** "Surrealist Dislocation" (Objects in impossible places).

---

## ❌ Anti-Patterns (NEVER DO)

1.  **Overlay Text:** Text must be *inside* the 3D world (engraved, glowing, projected). Never floating on top like a Canva layer.
2.  **Flat Lighting:** There must always be a rim light or volumetric fog.
3.  **Perfect Surfaces:** Add `dust`, `scratches`, or `grain` to prove reality.
4.  **Generic Blue:** Use **Electric Violet** or **Cyan**, never "Twitter Blue".
