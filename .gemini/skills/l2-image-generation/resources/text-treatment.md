# L2 SYSTEMS // TEXT TREATMENT PROTOCOLS

> **CORE LAW:** Text must be **PHYSICAL**. It must strictly obey the laws of physics, material, and lighting.
> **BANNED:** No "floating overlays". No weak "holographic projections" (unless it's a physical screen).

---

## 1. THE ENGRAVING (Permanent Statements)
**Best for:** Manifestos, Negative Statements ("Não construímos sites"), Core Values.
**The Look:** Text is physically CARVED or ETCHED into heavy materials (Obsidian, Titanium, Matte Black Aluminum).
*   **Keywords:** `Deeply engraved`, `etched into surface`, `carved stone`, `debossed metal`, `industrial milling`.
*   **Lighting:** Rim lighting catches the edges of the carving. Slight internal glow *inside* the grooves (magma/energy vein).
*   **Font Style:** Heavy, Industrial, Sans-Serif (Inter Black, Archivo Black).

## 2. THE HARDWARE SCREEN (Data & Lists)
**Best for:** Educational lists (5 Signs), Metrics, Diagnostics, Code.
**The Look:** A physical piece of hardware (OLED Panel, Server Diagnostic Screen) embedded in metal. NOT a floating UI.
*   **Keywords:** `Embedded OLED screen`, `server rack display`, `macro photography of pixels`, `dot matrix`, `terminal font`, `monitor glare`, `fingerprints on glass`.
*   **Lighting:** The light comes *from* the pixels.
*   **Font Style:** Monospace, Console, Terminal, VCR OSD.

## 3. PHYSICAL LIGHT (Brand & CTA)
**Best for:** Logos, Main Headers ("L2 SYSTEMS"), Launch announcements.
**The Look:** The text object IS the light source. Neon tubes, Plasma channels, Liquid Cooling pipes shaped like letters.
*   **Keywords:** `3D neon installation`, `glass tubes filled with plasma`, `liquid cooling pipes`, `channel letter signage`, `backlit stencil cut`, `negative space light`.
*   **Lighting:** Casts real shadows on the wall/floor. Illuminates the fog.
*   **Font Style:** Thick strokes (to allow volume), Crystalline or Glass tubing.

## 4. INTERNAL HUD (Technical Context)
**Best for:** Machine labels, Status indicators ("ONLINE"), Secondary text.
**The Look:** Text that is part of the machine's internal optics or printed on the chassis.
*   **Keywords:** `Internal optical readout`, `printed on chassis`, `laser marked`, `warning label aesthetic`, `machine status indicator`.
*   **Lighting:** Subtle, functional glow.
*   **Font Style:** Technical, Small, Precision.

---

## ❌ ANTI-PATTERNS (DO NOT DO)
*   **The "Force Ghost":** Weak, semi-transparent blue text floating in mid-air.
*   **The "Youtube Thumbnail":** Big bold impact font overlaid on top of the image in Photoshop.
*   **The "Weak Beam":** A projector shooting a 2D text sprite.
*   **Thin Fonts:** Light/Regular weights disappear in dark modes. Use BOLD/BLACK weights.
